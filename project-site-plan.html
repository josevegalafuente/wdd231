Project Site Plan

Hiking Tarija, Safe Trails & Scenic Landscapes (Tarija, Bolivia)

1. Site Name

Hiking Tarija

This name instantly communicates the topic (hiking) and the location (Tarija). It is simple, memorable, and clearly aligned with the purpose of the website: guiding visitors to safe trails and scenic outdoor places.

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of Hiking Tarija is to provide local residents and tourists with a fast, reliable guide to discover safe hiking routes and beautiful scenic viewpoints in and around Tarija. The site will offer trail summaries, difficulty levels, estimated time and distance, safety recommendations, and simple planning tips (what to bring, best seasons, and how to reach each location).

The website will focus on clarity and accessibility so users can quickly decide where to go, prepare properly, and enjoy Tarija’s landscapes responsibly.

3. Scenarios

Target audience questions the site will answer:

  • Which beginner-friendly hiking trails near Tarija are safe and easy to access?
  • What trail offers the best viewpoints for sunrise or sunset photography?
  • How long does each hike take and what equipment should I bring?
  • Are there family-friendly outdoor spots with picnic areas and clear directions?

5. Typography

  • Optional accent/utility: Monospace for short technical notes and labels.
